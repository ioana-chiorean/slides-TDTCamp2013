WoMoz is…

the community of women and men from mozilla and other open source projects that support the participation of women in open source projects.

Goals

  • Increase gender diversity in mozilla community and other FLOSS projects.
  • Given the actual figures - at the moment this means more women.

Why diversity is important?

Uniformity leads to boredom (Antoine Houdar)

Statistics…

  • Over 50% of humans are .. females
  • In FLOSS the number is way less..
  • Half of our/your potential users are women
  • A product is the image of its creator..

How can women contribute to FLOSS

  • Bugs reporting
  • Code (imagine that - women can code!)
  • Design
  • Documentation
  • Event organization
  • L10n - localization
  • Marketing
  • Support
  • ... yes... pretty much everything!

Even more (not just a another man)...

  • Fresh new eye
  • Organization
  • Feminine touch (child safe too)
  • .. Proud to be a women!

How to change things..

  • No discrimination!
  • - no sexist jokes!
  • - don't act surprise they are round
  • - prepare your event for them (ex. tshirts)
  • - encourage their participation - special programs!

What will be keep on doing..

  • Keep Womoz alive (new logo/website)
  • Make presentations to different events
  • Actively recruit women already contributing (show they are not alone)
  • Workshops (apps, webmaker)
  • Less talk, more actions/changes !
